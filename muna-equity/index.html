MNA Equity Statement

Our vision:  diversity, equity, and inclusion are natural parts of what we do to be good neighbors. To honor that vision, we express those qualities in common neighborly terms: openness, fairness, and welcoming.

We’ve added safety to the list, because the neighborhood needs to be safe in order for its residents to feel like being open, fair, and welcoming. Safety is especially important to those who have experienced traumatically unsafe treatment from systemic unfair discrimination.

We make special efforts to include and support communities that have been specially mistreated. And that’s top priority. At the same time we are good neighbors with communities that have been well-treated, and call on them to help to build a more open, fair, welcoming, and safe neighborhood for all.
